Honey Milk Papaya Boat

April 4, 2021 by Mandy

Yield: 2 servings
Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 10 minutes
Total time: 15 minutes

How about another easy self-care recipe for glowy skin? Papayas have been used in Chinese desserts and soups for many generations for it’s skin nourishing benefits. This recipe is incredibly easy to make. The steamed papaya fruit tastes like a juicy melon and is such a delight. Simply cut the papaya in half, carefully scooping out all the seeds. Add your favorite milk and flavor it to taste with some honey. I added goji Berries for a boost of antioxidants. Steam for 8 minutes and voila! Next time, I am thinking about switching it up by adding some cinnamon. That’s the thing I love most about recipes. They are meant to be a reference and you can choose your own adventure, flavoring it to your particular taste. 😊

Hope you have a relaxing weekend before the hustle and bustle of the new week begins.

Ingredients

  • 1 papaya
  • 1 tbsp of honey
  • 1 tbsp goji berries
  • 1/2 (+/-) cup of your favorite milk, depending on size of papaya
Previous Next

Directions

  1. Cut the papaya in half, lengthwise.
  2. Carefully scoop out all the seeds.
  3. Place two small bowls on a flat plate or tray so the papaya can be held in place.
  4. Pour 1/4 (+/-) cup of milk into each papaya, until it is almost filled.
  5. Add 1/2 tbsp goji berries and drizzle 1/2 tbsp of honey into each papaya.
  6. Steam for 8 minutes. Let it rest for 2 minutes and enjoy!
