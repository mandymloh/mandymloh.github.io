Agedashi Tofu

July 15, 2021 by Mandy

Skip to Ingredients List
Yield: 4 servings
Prep time: 10 minutes
Cook time: 15 minutes
Total time: 25 minutes

Ingredients


    For the Fried Tofu

  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 1/2 cup of corn starch (or more to coat the tofu)
  • Olive oil for frying

    • For the Dashi Broth

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tsp of dashi powder
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of mirin
  • 3/4 cup of bonito flakes

    • Optional Toppings

  • 2 tsp of daikon radish (grated)
  • Bonito flakes
  • Sliced green onions
  • Seaweed strips
Previous Next

Directions



In a pot, bring 1 cup of water to boil and add in all the dashi broth ingredients.
Let the broth simmer on low heat for 15 minutes and pour through a mesh sieve. Set aside.



Gently press the tofu with a paper towel to get rid of excess water.
Cut into equal sized rectangular pieces. I cut mine into eight.
Carefully dip each tofu into corn starch, covering all sides.
Heat up 1 cup of olive oil in a pan on medium high heat and pan fry the tofu on both sides for 5 minutes or more until golden brown.



Assemble the fried tofu neatly in a serving bowl.
Add toppings and pour dashi broth around the tofu.
