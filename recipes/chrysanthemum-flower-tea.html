Chrysanthemum Flower Tea with Red Dates and Goji Berries

March 31, 2021 by Mandy

The Chrysanthemum was first cultivated in China and has been described in writings as early as the 15th Century B.C.1 It has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for many centuries. Chrysanthemum has a flowery herbal taste. Similar to Chamomile, lovers of this tea say that it has a calming effect and reduces inflammation. To pack even more nutrients, I made this tea with dried red dates, goji berries, and honey. Red dates are also known for anti-inflammatory benefits and the best part, improving overall skin tone and complexion! Goji berries, a superfruit, are highly packed with antioxidants. You can flavor the tea to your taste, adding more honey as you like.

Yield: 4 servings
Total time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of boiling water
  • 1 tbsp of Chrysanthemum flowers
  • 1 tbsp goji berries
  • 4 red dates (pits removed)
  • Honey (flavor to taste)
For Serving
  • Ice (optional)
Previous Next

Directions

  1. To a small pot, add the Chrysanthemum flowers, red dates, and goji berries.
  2. Add 4 cups of hot boiling water and let it steep for 10 minutes.
  3. Strain the tea through a sieve.
  4. Add honey, flavor accordingly to your taste.
