Homemade Chocolate Chip Cookies

April 23, 2021 by Mandy

Skip to Ingredients List
Yield: 6 servings
Prep time: 45 minutes
Cook time: 20 minutes
Total time: 1 hour and 5 minutes

This is a very delicious homemade chocolate chip cookie recipe that tastes similar to the ones sold at the Famous Amos cookie shops. When I lived in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, my favorite cookie shop was Famous Amos. I loved the smell of freshly baked chocolate cookies in the air. I was so happy to visit Kuala Lumpur 2 years ago and bought so many bags of cookies before I left. Recently, I tried a recipe that has gone viral in Malaysia. With this recipe, you can have homemade chocolate chip cookies and even have enough to share with friends!

Can the cookies be customized?

Definitely! I love doubling the recipe to make multiple flavors like white chocolate macadamia nut, adding dark cocoa powder or matcha powder with white chocolate chips.

Do you need a mixer?

Having one certainly makes the job a lot easier. I use a kitchen aid mixer for this chocolate chip cooking recipe. You can also mix it by hand with a spoon.

Ingredients


  • 250g of unsalted butter at room temperature (about 2-1/2 4-oz sticks of butter)
  • 1-1/4 cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanilla essence
  • 1 egg (room temperature)
  • 2-1/2 cups of all purpose flour (sifted)
  • 1/2 cup of corn starch (sifted)
  • 1-1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • 6 tbsp of olive oil
  • 2 cups of dark chocolate chips

Directions


  1. Mix together 250g of unsalted butter and 1-1/4 cups of brown sugar until well incorporated.
  2. Add 1 egg and 1 tsp of vanilla essence. Continue mixing.
  3. Add 2-1/2 cups of sifted all purpose flour, 1/2 cup of corn starch, 1-1/2 tsp of baking powder, 6 tbsp of oil until well combined.
  4. Toss in 2 cups of dark chocolate chips.
  5. Cover the cookie dough and allow it to rest for 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  6. Once the dough has finished resting, spoon 1 tsp of cookie dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake at 300°F for 20 minutes.
